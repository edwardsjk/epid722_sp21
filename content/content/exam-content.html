The exam will be posted to the Teams site on Friday 3/19 at 4pm. You must turn in your completed exam (to Teams) by 5pm on Tuesday 3/23.

Submission instructions: Please submit your exam as a PDF. Name your document using your UNC PID in the format PID_MIDTERM.PDF, and do not include any other identifying information on the document you submit. Separately, please upload a signed version of the provided honor pledge. You may download a copy of the honor pledge from the EPID 722 Teams site.

For this exam, you may reference any textbooks, notes, or other materials. However, discussion of exam questions with anyone before all exams are submitted is forbidden. Please do not share electronic or print versions of this exam with anyone.

For calculations, please show all work needed to arrive at your answer. For short answer questions, please keep answers concise and be sure to answer all parts of the question.

For clarification questions, please email the entire instructional team with “EPID 722 MIDTERM Q” in the subject line.

You may print the exam document, complete by hand, scan, and upload OR type your responses, convert to PDF, and upload. Please contact the TAs or Instructors with any questions regarding format or submission.
